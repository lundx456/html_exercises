Our Happy Tails Team: Learn More ⇩

By working with committed volunteers, foster homes, local veterinarians, trainers and boarding facilities, Happy Tails is able to rescue dogs and cats in need. We work for providing them with loving, temporary care until a well-matched, carefully screened forever home can be found.

Our Mission: to find beauty in the most incomprehensible places and the otherwise homely faces. It is our gift to see beyond the dirt, terror, sadness and the defeat to find the true soul that lies within. We are rescue." We are... HAPPY TAILS
